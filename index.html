Gmail

How to make and use your own Gmail account.

Hello everyone, my name is Rob. I am a 3rd year Batchelor of Information Technology student at the Otago Polytech. My goal with this video is to show you how to create your own Gmail account for email and how to use it. The first step is to open an internet browser and navigate to a search engine. If you are unsure how to do this, please go back and watch our other video on internet browsers. Now to begin. As you can see, I have google chrome open and I am currently on the google search page. From here, I will click on the search bar. If you follow along and type Gmail followed by the enter key. The first option for me I Gmail so click on this link This page has the option to create a new account as well as sign in if you already have an account. For today we will click on the create account button. Here is where we begin to create the account you will need to enter your first name and last name. The next step is making the username or email address. As you can see the @gmail.com is already filled out for us. To pick your username it is important to get something that will be easy for you to remember. An idea for this is firstname.lastname@gmail.com. This might not be an option for you if you have a common name like John Smith. To get around this there are some things we could do. Like adding the year that you were born to the end of your username. Or substituting a nickname for your first name. Its important to make sure that you will be able to recall the email address at a later date. If you think that you might forget it now is a good time to pause this video while you make a note of it somewhere. The next step is obviously setting a secure password. This is also very important to choose something that you will be able to remember easily. Some examples of this are a place you went to and the year, for example munich2012. Alternatively, the name of a pet and the year you got them. These are just a few ideas of what can be used. Again, it is important to remember this so if you think you will struggle to remember this please feel free to pause the video and make a note of it somewhere as well. Now after we have completed this we will set up some recovery options for incase you forget your password. If you have a cell phone, you can enter it here to link your email account to your cell phone. If you forget your email or password this will mean that, you can send a recovery option to the phone number listed here. If you do not have, a phone number but have a close relative that is happy to be your recovery email address you can place there email in the recovery section below the phone number option. It is important to ask them if they are happy to do this first to avoid upsetting anybody. If you donâ€™t wish to do any of these options that is ok to. The next step will be to enter your D.O.B in the appropriate section followed by your gender. Then you press next and will be taken to the next page, which consists of the terms and conditions. Please pause the video and take your time to read these. After you have read, the terms you will be prompted to press agree and from there will have set up your email account.
